Steve Jobs

About

Steve Jobs (1955–2011) was an American entrepreneur, co-founder of Apple Inc. He revolutionized personal computers, music players, and smartphones.

Timeline

  1. 1955 – Born in San Francisco
  2. 1976 – Co-founded Apple
  3. 1984 – Launched Macintosh
  4. 2007 – Introduced iPhone
  5. 2011 – Passed away

Key Contributions

  • Apple Macintosh
  • iPod
  • iPhone
  • Pixar Animation
